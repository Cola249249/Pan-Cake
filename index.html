Pan Cake Receipe

Cooking Time In 15Minutes

Serves For People

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tablespoons of sugar
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 spoon of salt
  • 1 cup of buttermailk
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Tablespoons of melted butter

Instructions

  • In large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  • In another bowl, mix buttermailk, egg, and melted butter.
  • Pour the wet inggredients into the dry Ingredients and stir until combined.
  • Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease with butter.
  • Pour 1/4 cup of butter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  • Flip the pancake and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  • Repeat with the remaining butter, serve warm with syyrup or topping of choice.
